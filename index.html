The Floor Is Java

Cafe and Coffee Shop

Welcome!

If you're like us, you probably spent hours of your childhood pretending the floor was lava and frantically trying to avoid touching it at all costs. Well, we're here to tell you that the floor is no longer lava - it's java! And it's hot, it's delicious, and it's waiting for you at our coffee shop.

Located in the heart of downtown, we're a locally owned and operated afe that's all about bringing you the best java around. From creamy lattes to bold espressos, we've got every type of coffee you could ever want. And if you're not in the mood for coffee, we've also got a variety of teas, pastries, and sandwiches to choose from. So come on in and join us at The Floor is Java. We promise there's no actual lava involved. Just some seriously good coffee and a cozy atmosphere that's perfect for catching up with friends or just relaxing with a book. We can't wait to serve you!
